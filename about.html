Hey there!

I'm a roboticist with a Bachelors degree in engineering from BIT, Mesra (2011) and an M.S. degree in computer science from the University of Southern California, Los Angeles (2014).

I specialize in robotics, decision making and multi agent systems. I have previously worked on mobile, humanoid and interactive robots. I enjoy solving problems in these spaces and have collaborated with teams of varying sizes and maturities. My experience includes aspects of product development that range from initial problem decomposition, to architecture selection, feature development and system validation.

My research interests include unmanned aerial systems, robot locomotion, remote sensing, multi agent path planning, neural networks, target tracking and localization.
